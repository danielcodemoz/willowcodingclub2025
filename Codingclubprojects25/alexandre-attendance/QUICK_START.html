üìã Main Sections

Dashboard

View overall statistics, recent attendance, and quick access to all features.

Students

Add, edit, delete, search students. Import bulk students from CSV or export to CSV.

Sections

Create and manage different sections (Art, Sports, Science, etc.). Each section has a unique code.

Attendance

Mark daily attendance for students by section. Four statuses available:

  • Present: Student attended
  • Absent: Student did not attend
  • Late: Student arrived late
  • Excused: Student has valid excuse

Reports

Generate detailed attendance reports filtered by section and date range. Export reports to CSV.

About Dwayne Johnson

Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson is an American actor, producer, and retired professional wrestler known for his charismatic presence and action-packed roles.

He rose to fame as a WWE wrestling superstar before becoming one of Hollywood’s highest-paid actors. His notable movies include Jumanji, Fast & Furious series, and Moana.

Beyond entertainment, Dwayne is also an entrepreneur and philanthropist, inspiring millions with his dedication and work ethic.

Fun Fact: Before wrestling, he briefly played college football and was signed by the Canadian Football League.

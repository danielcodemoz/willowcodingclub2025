About Zendaya

Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman, known mononymously as Zendaya, is an American actress, singer, and producer. She gained fame for her role in the Disney Channel series Shake It Up and later starred in critically acclaimed projects like Euphoria and the Spider-Man films.

Zendaya is admired not only for her acting but also for her activism, fashion influence, and advocacy for diversity and inclusion.

She has received several awards including an Emmy for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series.

Fun Fact: Zendaya started her career as a model and backup dancer before transitioning to acting.

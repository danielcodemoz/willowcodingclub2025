About Our Library

Our History

Founded in 1985, our local library has been serving the community for over 40 years. What started as a small collection of donated books has grown into a vibrant community hub with over 50,000 books, magazines, and digital resources.

Our mission has always been to provide free access to information, promote literacy, and foster a love of reading among people of all ages.

Services

  • Book lending (up to 20 items per person)
  • Digital resources and e-books
  • Research assistance
  • Community meeting rooms
  • Children's reading programs
  • Computer and internet access
